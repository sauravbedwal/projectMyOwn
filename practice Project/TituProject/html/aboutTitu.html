Titu is the owner of a small, casual dining restaurant called "Titu's Eats." He has been in the
                food industry for over 10 years and has always had a passion for cooking and experimenting with different

                recipe or helping out servers during busy hours. He is also very involved in the local community, regularly
                hosting fundraising events and supporting local charities.
